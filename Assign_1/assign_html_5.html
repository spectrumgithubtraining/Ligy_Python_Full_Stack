Instructions:

  1. Prepare the ingredients for the recipe, including flour, eggs, sugar, butter, and baking powder.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a baking pan with butter or cooking spray.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients: flour and baking powder. Mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and add sugar. Mix until the sugar is dissolved.
  5. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring gently to form a smooth batter.
